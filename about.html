About Me

Vishnu Suresh

Programmer / Game Designer

Hi! I’m Vishnu Suresh, a Programmer and Game Designer based in Vancouver, BC, Canada.


I specialize in gameplay mechanics, systems development, and solving technical challenges to create immersive, interactive experiences that are both fun and performant.


Outside of games, I stay active by running, swimming, and hiking, and I bring that same energy and curiosity to everything I do. I'm always eager to learn something new, no matter the subject. Every challenge is an opportunity, and games are where I have the most fun pushing myself creatively and technically.


During my time at Vancouver Film School, I worked on two major projects - Train Wreck and Calamity Chasm - along with a few game jam titles. Feel free to check them out on itch.io
